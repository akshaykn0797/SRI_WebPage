About Me

Greetings! My name is Harshitha, and I am a passionate graduate student pursuing a Masters in Computer Science at Old Dominion University. Originally hailing from the vibrant city of Nalgonda, India, I have embarked on an exciting journey at ODU, starting with the Spring 2023 batch. With a strong affinity for Data Science, AI, and Web Development, I am dedicated to unraveling the limitless possibilities that lie within these fascinating fields.

Alongside my academic journey, I consider myself both an artist and an avid traveler. For me, painting is not just a creative outlet; it's a means to document and share the profound experiences I've had during my journeys around the world. From the bustling streets of New York to the serene landscapes of Virginia, each place I visit becomes a canvas for my imagination. Through my art, I aim to capture the vibrant tapestry of cultures and landscapes that have left an indelible mark on my soul. Join me on this visual voyage, where the brush strokes on my canvas mirror the colors and emotions of my globetrotting adventures.
